Our Story

More For Good is a boutique consultancy agency located in Leominster, MA, founded by Sarah Shields in 2017. More For Good specializes in the support of nonprofits and values based organizations.

Sarah brings nearly 20 years of nonprofit support, marketing and leadership development experience to the table, and has done work for churches, para-church, youth and art organizations, to name a few. When she’s not helping nonprofits get more done, she enjoys spending time with her family, hiking on the many local trails, making music and baking.
